About Me

My name is Elizabeth Gonzalez, I am a current third year PhD candidate at Penn State, where I develop stellar variability mitigation techniques for extreme precision radial velocity. I completed my undergraduate at Columbia University, where I gained research experience in exoplanets and star formation. I am very passionate about doing research in astronomy and plan to earn my PhD degree in astronomy and astrophysics. As a first-generation Latina, I understand the challenges that come with being underrepresented in STEM. Thus far as a PhD student, I have been involved in organizing multiple science events at nearby public schools and community outreach events.
